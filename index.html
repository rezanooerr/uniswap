Easy method to earn Passive Income using Uniswap

Front running, also known as tailgating, is the practice of entering into an equity trade, option, futures contract, derivative, or security-based swap to capitalize on advance, nonpublic knowledge of a large pending transaction that will influence the price of the underlying security.

The term “front-running” refers to the process when someone uses technology or market advantage to get prior knowledge of upcoming transactions.
